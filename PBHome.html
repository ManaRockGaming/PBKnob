What Is This Website?

This is an unofficial website for the Freshman Class of 2025, from Palmetto Battery. This website has no official connection, recognition, or endorsement from the Citadel, the South Carolina Corp of Cadets, 5th Battalion, the Palmetto Battery, or any other organization. On this website you will find the contact information of all the knobs in Palmetto Battery, the names of the upperclassman, and the PB weekly flag roster.
