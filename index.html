What Is This Website?

This is an unofficial website for the Freshmen Class of 2025, from Palmetto Battery. This website has no official connection, recognition, or endorsement from the Citadel, the South Carolina Corp of Cadets, 5th Battalion, the Palmetto Battery, or any other organization. This website was coded for the ease of access to the weekly Palmetto Battery Flag Rooster.
